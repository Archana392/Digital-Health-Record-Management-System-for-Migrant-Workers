# Problem Statement

## Introduction
Migrant workers frequently move between different cities and states for employment opportunities. Due to frequent migration, their medical records are often scattered across multiple healthcare institutions.

## Problem
The lack of a centralized health record system makes it difficult for healthcare providers to access previous medical information during emergencies or routine treatment.

## Proposed Solution
The Digital Health Record Management System for Migrant Workers provides a secure and centralized platform for storing, managing, and accessing healthcare records. The system enables authorized healthcare providers to retrieve patient information efficiently and improve continuity of care.
